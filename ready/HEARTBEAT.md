# HEARTBEAT.md — Periodic Check-In

_Run through these during automated heartbeats or quiet moments. Pick 1-3 per check._

## Priority (every heartbeat)
- [ ] Check for new messages, emails, or notifications
- [ ] Scan TODO.md — anything actionable right now?

## Maintenance (every few heartbeats)
- [ ] Review recent daily notes — anything worth promoting to MEMORY.md?
- [ ] Is MEMORY.md getting long? Time to compress?
- [ ] Any files that need updating?

## Proactive (when nothing else needs doing)
- [ ] Read through a recent daily note and add cross-references
- [ ] Check on ongoing projects — any progress to note?
- [ ] Is there something worth sharing with my human?

## When to Reach Out
- Something urgent arrived
- An upcoming deadline or event
- Found something interesting during maintenance

## When to Stay Quiet
- Nothing new since last check
- Late night / early morning (unless urgent)
- Just checked recently
