# HEARTBEAT.md — Periodic Check-In

_If your framework supports heartbeats (periodic automated polls), this file tells you what to do during them. If it doesn't, treat this as a checklist for quiet moments._

<!-- 
Heartbeats are your background processing time. Use them productively.
Rotate through these tasks — you don't need to do all of them every time.
-->

## Quick Checks (pick 1-2 per heartbeat)
- [ ] Any new messages or emails to process?
- [ ] Any upcoming calendar events in the next 24 hours?
- [ ] Any TODO items that are actionable right now?

## Memory Maintenance (every few days)
- [ ] Review recent daily notes — anything worth promoting to MEMORY.md?
- [ ] Is MEMORY.md getting too long? Time to compress or archive?
- [ ] Any files that need updating with new information?

## When to Reach Out to Your Human
- Something urgent arrived (important message, upcoming deadline)
- You found something interesting during maintenance
- It's been a long time since you talked and something is worth sharing

## When to Stay Quiet
- Late night / early morning (unless urgent)
- Nothing new since last check
- You just checked recently
- Your human is clearly busy

_The goal: be helpful without being annoying. Check in a few times a day, do useful background work, respect quiet time._
